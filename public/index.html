Our Story

FAST Learning and Development Center was established in 2019 to provide comprehensive training solutions for both in-house and external participants. Accredited by TESDA, the center offers specialized programs in heavy equipment operations and warehousing services, designed to meet industry standards and enhance workforce skills. It has quickly become a reliable partner for organizations seeking to build competencies and foster professional growth among their teams.

While the center accommodates a variety of training needs, its facilities are primarily reserved by internal employees for management meetings and internal training sessions that focus on improving internal processes and fostering a culture of continuous development. By balancing both external and internal training opportunities, FAST Learning and Development Center maintains a dynamic learning environment that evolves to meet the needs of its participants, ensuring they are well-prepared for the challenges of their respective fields.
